Manage your miners from home

Let us make sure your miners are up, and you'll do the management

The times when you could have a few miners running at home is history. Let Fjellkjede worry about the electricity, network, cooling and operations of your miners.

Now, the challenge for small- and medium sized miners are to find an affordable and reliable place to place the miners. This is where Fjellkjede comes in. We make sure your miners are up, and so that you can keep an eye on them with VPN.

Our VPN offering include both software and hardware measures to keep communication secure. Key components in this easy to use VPN offering is Zerotier and an OrangePi.
