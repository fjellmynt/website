About us

Fjellkjede AS is a Norwegian company specializing in blockchain technology. Our mission is to enable organisations, and individuals, to utilize blockchain technology to the max. This means that the company will offer products and services at all levels of the value chain. Staring from the raw maintenance of the blockchain, all the way to providing utilities to interact with the blockchain.

Fjellkjede has multiple facilities designed to host blockchain miners. Miners can be ASIC or GPU type to participate in public blockchains, but we also offer hosting of miners in closed proprietary blockchains.

Additionally Fjellkjede focuses on creating utility apps to interact and value-add block chains of any kind.

Whether you are an individual mining cryptocurrency or a business maintaining your own blockchain, Fjellkjede AS is here to help.

The company was founded in 2018 by 6 people passionate about the opportunities blockchains and crypto technology represents. The company is privately held, with solid investors. The team has vast experience from the tech industry very relevant to the new era of decentralized systems.

We provide:

  • Miner hosting facilities
  • Miner orchestration software - DvergOS
  • Utility Apps for consumers
  • Hash power lease
  • Professional Services
