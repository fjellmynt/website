About Fjellkjede

Fjellkjede is a Norwegian company specializing in blockchain technology, computing power farms and value added services built on decentralized systems. Our mission is to enable organisations, and individuals, to utilize blockchain technology to the max. This means that the company will offer products and services at all levels of the value chain. Staring from the raw maintenance of the blockchain, all the way to providing utilities to interact with the blockchain.

At Fjellkjde, we are committed to maintaining the trust and confidence of our customers. Below we give detailed information on when and why we collect your personal information, how we use it, the limited conditions under which we may disclose it to others and how we keep it secure.

Website cookies

When you access any of our web poperties, we use cookies to track usage and to make the web properties function better for you as a user (session information).

When someone visits our web properties we use a third party service, Google Analytics, to collect standard internet log information and details of visitor behaviour patterns. We do this to find out things such as the number of visitors to the various parts of the site. This information is only processed in a way which does not identify anyone. We do not make, and do not allow Google to make, any attempt to find out the identities of those visiting our website.

Mailing lists

As part of the registration process for new customers and opt-ins for newsletters, we collect personal information. We use that information for a couple of reasons: to tell you about stuff you’ve asked us to tell you about; to contact you if we need to obtain or provide additional information; to check our records are right and to check every now and then that you’re happy and satisfied. We don’t rent or trade email lists with other organisations and businesses.

We use a third-party provider, MailChimp, to deliver our newsletter. We gather statistics around email opening and clicks using industry standard technologies to help us monitor and improve our e-newsletter. For more information, please see MailChimp’s privacy notice. You can unsubscribe to general mailings at any time of the day or night by clicking the unsubscribe link at the bottom of any of our emails or by emailing our data protection officer (ping@fjellkjede.no).

Third Parties

For our customers we use a system (Conta Faktura) for sending invoices. In order to do so we must share some personal information with them to enable you to pay for the service you purchased. We’ve signed a (Data Processing Agreement)[https://hjelp.conta.no/databehandleravtale/] with them in accordance with GDPR (see : https://contagroup.no/personvernerklaering/). We only keep your personal data there for as long as you’re our customer, or as long as we’re required by law.

Access to your personal data

You are entitled to view, amend, or delete the personal information that we hold. Email your request to our data protection officer Jon Arne Sæterås (ping@fjellkjede.no).

Changes to this Privacy Agreement

We may change this privacy agreement from time to time. If we think they changes are important enough, we’ll alert you.
